Overview

Citation Fuels brings decades of specialized experience to the construction of petroleum infrastructure. From ground-up retail fuel station builds to large-scale industrial storage tank installations, our team handles every aspect of the project. We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, within budget, and with a steadfast commitment to safety and environmental compliance.

Our Construction Capabilities

Retail Fueling Stations

Complete installation of underground storage tanks (USTs), dispensers, piping, and canopy structures for modern retail sites.

Commercial & Industrial Bulk Systems

Design and construction of high-capacity fuel storage systems for fleet trucking operations and power generation facilities.

Civil & Structural Engineering

Integrated civil works, including concrete pads, drainage systems, and structural framing for complex fueling environments.

Construction FAQs

Is Citation Fuels licensed for hazardous material handling?

Absolutely. Our teams are fully certified to handle, install, and test systems involving hazardous petroleum products in accordance with state and federal regulations.

What is the typical timeline for a new fueling station build?

While timelines vary based on scope and permitting, a standard retail build typically ranges from 12 to 24 weeks from site preparation to commissioning.

Do you provide environmental impact assessments?

Yes, as part of our construction process, we conduct thorough assessments to ensure compliance with environmental preservation laws.
